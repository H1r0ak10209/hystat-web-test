Privacy Policy

HyStat Co., Ltd. (hereinafter "the Company") recognizes the protection of personal information as an important responsibility, and complies with the Act on the Protection of Personal Information (Personal Information Protection Act) and other related laws and regulations to properly manage and protect personal information.

1. Personal Information We Collect

The Company may collect personal information in the following circumstances.

  • Inquiries via contact form: name, email address, and inquiry content

2. Purpose of Use

Personal information collected will be used for the following purposes.

  • Responding to inquiries
  • Providing information about our services
  • Responding to matters required by law

3. Third-Party Disclosure

The Company will not provide personal information to third parties except in the following cases.

  • When the individual has given consent
  • When disclosure is required by law
  • When necessary to protect a person's life, body, or property

4. Security Management

The Company takes appropriate security measures to prevent the leakage, loss, or damage of collected personal information. The Company also provides appropriate supervision of employees who handle personal information.

5. Use of Cookies and Analytics Tools

Our website may use cookies and access analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) for the purpose of improving our services. These tools collect data but do not include personally identifiable information. If you do not wish to use cookies, you can disable them through your browser settings.

6. Disclosure, Correction, and Deletion

You have the right to request disclosure, correction, or deletion of your personal information held by the Company. Please contact us at the contact information below. After verifying your identity, we will respond within a reasonable period of time.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

The Company may revise this Privacy Policy in response to changes in laws or service content. Any revisions will be posted on this page and will take effect on the date of posting.

8. Contact

For inquiries regarding the handling of personal information, please contact us at the following.
