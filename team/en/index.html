Founders

Takayuki Kiura

木浦 尊之

Representative Director & CEO

After graduating from Kyoto University's Faculty of Economics, he joined Mitsubishi Estate. Following completion of MIT's Master's program in Real Estate, he joined Heitman's Chicago headquarters — one of the leading U.S. real estate investment advisors — where he focused on U.S. REIT analysis. Upon returning to Japan, he established Heitman's Tokyo office as the firm's first Asian outpost and was appointed Managing Director. He covered institutional investors and pension funds across major Asian markets including Japan, South Korea, and Singapore, and led the firm's Asian marketing efforts as Equity Partner. After departing in 2025, he founded BASE-K. Holds ARES Master and MRICS designations.

Hiroaki Matsushima

松島 弘哲

Director

Concurrently serves as a Visiting Researcher at Stanford University's US-Asia Technology Management Center. Graduated from the School of Political Science and Economics at Meiji University, and studied abroad at the University of Georgia. Engaged in B2B sales at IT system development and data processing companies, as well as HENNGE, a SaaS security company, handling everything from identifying customer challenges to proposing solutions. Researches PropTech (real estate technology) and strategic alliances with the Silicon Valley startup ecosystem.

Jiro Yoshida

吉田 二郎

Founder

Trustee and Smeal College of Business Professor of Real Estate at Penn State University. Concurrently serves as Specially Appointed Professor at the University of Tokyo, and as a board member of the International Real Estate Society and the Asian Real Estate Society; also holds research appointments at Columbia University and Stanford University. Has previously served as Visiting Professor at MIT, Visiting Assistant Professor at UC Berkeley, Specially Appointed Associate Professor at Hitotsubashi University and the University of Tokyo, and as a researcher at the Ministry of Finance and the Bank of Japan. Specializes in real estate economics, financial economics, and macroeconomics. Fulbright Scholar and recipient of the American Real Estate and Urban Economics Association Dissertation Award. Holds B.Eng. from the University of Tokyo, M.S. from MIT, and M.A. and Ph.D. from UC Berkeley.

Chihiro Shimizu

清水 千弘

Founder

Professor at the Graduate School of Social Data Science, Hitotsubashi University. Ph.D. (Environmental Studies). Has held positions at Reitaku University, Nihon University, and the University of Tokyo, among others, before assuming his current role. Has also served as Visiting Professor at the University of British Columbia, National University of Singapore, and the University of Hong Kong. Holds numerous concurrent research appointments, including Fellow at Recruit AI Research Lab and researcher at MIT. Specializes in index theory, big data analytics, real estate economics, and sports data science.
